NUMBERS GAME

RULES

  • THIS IS A SIMPLE GAME FOR CHILDREN ABOUT FINDING AND MATCHING NUMBERS FROM 0 - 9 AND 100.
  • ALL VALUES ARE GENREATED BY THE COMPUTER.
  • FOR EVERY SUCCESSFUL AND UNSUCCESSFUL MATCHING THERE IS POP UP SHOWN ABOUT RESULT.
  • AFTER EVERY SUCCESSFUL MATCHING THE VALUES AUTOMATICALLY REFRESHED.
